About Me

I am currently a Ph.D candidate of Software Engineering in South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China. My supervisor is Prof. Mingkui Tan. I also work as an intern in Tencent AI Lab (since Sep 2019, ongoing), and here I am co-supervised by Dr. Jiaxiang Wu and Dr. Peilin Zhao. My research interests include neural architecture search, test-time adaptation for out-of-distribution generalization and deep domain adaptation.

Research Interests
